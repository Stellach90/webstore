With high protein, CRAVE Real Chicken Pate Perfect Portions Adult Wet Cat Food is inspired by a cat's natural diet. Real chicken is the first ingredient, and this protein-rich recipe provides cats with energy and supports lean bodies.

Key Benefits : High Protein

Benefits : High Protein

Item Number : 5291582

Life Stage : Adult

Nutritional Option : High Protein, No corn, wheat, or soy; Formulated to meet the nutritional levels established by the AAFCO Cat Food Nutrient Profiles for maintenance.

Weight : 75 g

Ingredients

Ingredients : Chicken, Chicken Liver, Pork Broth, Chicken Broth, Chicken Heart, Natural Flavour, Minerals, Guar Gum, Fish Oil (Naturally Preserved With Mixed Tochopherols), Carrageenan, Vitamins, Sodium Acid Pyrophosphate, Dried Tomatoes, Tetrasodium Pyrophosphate, Tapioca Starch, Salt, Sodium Hexametaphosphate, Taurine

Feeding instructions : Feed 4 servings daily per 3.6 kg (8 lb.) adult cat.
